Neurevolve simulates the evolution of organisms in a resource limited world. The behaviour of each organism is driven by a simple neural network.

Network

The neural networks in Neurevolve are very simple. They consist of a single ordered list of neurons. When a network is activated, it activates each neuron in the list in turn.

Each neuron contains:

  • A threshold.
  • Zero or more inputs from the world.
  • Zero or more weighted links to neurons earlier in the network.
  • Zero or one activities that might be performed in the world.
